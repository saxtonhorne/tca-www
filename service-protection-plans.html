When it comes to purchasing a new or pre-owned vehicle, one of the top concerns on a customer's mind is whether or not it will be a mechanical headache in the future.

The ability to have a plan in place, should a breakdown occur, is often the key to finalizing the transaction. Total Care Auto offers that plan.

With different levels of coverage, as well as many other available features and services designed to add maximum value and peace of mind to vehicle ownership, Total Care Auto delivers some of the most extensive offerings available in the market by protecting nearly every part of the vehicle and other repairs not typically covered by standard extended vehicle warranties.

Although, not every Total Care Auto vehicle service contract offers the same protection, terms, and benefits, we believe every customer deserves the absolute best and most complete coverage available. After all, you never know what might happen or go wrong with a vehicle - and peace of mind is priceless.
