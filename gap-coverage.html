Gap Coverage

Too often, many drivers learn later than sooner that a standard auto insurance policy might not provide all the coverage needed, especially in the case of a "total loss." While customers and borrowers alike have historically had the ability of meeting their known obligations, this unexpected loss of a vehicle could cause permanent damage to their credit history, as well as create associated dissatisfaction with the entire experience at the point of sale. It is with that in mind that Total Care Auto provides Gap Coverage service.

Here's how it works: If the value of a vehicle is less than the balance of an auto loan, a gap is created that typically isn't covered. With Total Care Auto Gap Coverage, that difference is completely handled, eliminating worry and financial headache.

By providing Total Care Auto Gap Coverage, you will essentially be providing your valued customers with the service and coverage needed to protect the full amount of their vehicle investment, as well as adding another source of revenue for your business. It is a win-win for everyone.
