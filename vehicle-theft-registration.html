Vehicle Theft Registration

Statistics say a vehicle is stolen every 23 seconds in the United States. Wouldn't you hate to see your hard-earned money just disappear without a trace? Even worse, have your life disrupted with the inconvenient loss of transportation.

With Total Care Auto Vehicle Theft Registration you get layers of protection such as body and window VIN etching to curb would-be thieves. This identification number is linked to our Total Care Vehicle Theft Registration database. This will not only help law enforcement officials track down the vehicle more easily but will help deter would-be thieves by identifying the vehicle as being protected by Total Care Vehicle Theft Registration.

And in the event that you are the victim of vehicle theft, Total Care Auto will pay you $2,500 should your vehicle be stolen and not recovered for 30 days.
