Vehicle Theft Registration

Statistics say a vehicle is stolen every 23 seconds in the United States. But the loss of a vehicle is only the tip of the iceberg compared to the disruption and inconvenience it can have in your customer's day-to-day life. Well, now you can take the easy action of protecting your investment - your customers - by helping them to protect theirs with Total Care Vehicle Theft Registration.

Total Care Vehicle Theft Registration offers layers of protection - such as body and window VIN etching - to curb would-be thieves. This identification number will be tagged or etched to select areas of the vehicle and linked to our Total Care Vehicle Theft Registration database. These tags can be removed at any time; however, the ID number will remain only to be seen with the use of a black light. That way, should the car go missing, law enforcement officials can easily track it down.

Window etching works in a very similar fashion with the only difference being that the identification number is permanently etched into the vehicle's glass. This will not only help in the recovery process, should it go missing, but will help deter would-be thieves by identifying the vehicle as being protected by Total Care Vehicle Theft Registration.

Even though it may be impossible to guarantee a vehicle will never be stolen, taking a few simple precautions to help protect your customer's investment can greatly reduce the chances for vehicle theft to occur.

And in the event that a customer is the victim of vehicle theft, Total Care Auto will pay them $2,500 that can easily be used toward the purchase of a new vehicle. We call it a win-win!
