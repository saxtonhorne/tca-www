ID Theft Protection

Your customers are vital to the success of your business. Demonstrating your commitment to them will keep them loyal to you. And what better way than providing services and solutions to help keep their personal information safe from the threat of identity theft? Stop identity theft in its tracks with Total Care Auto ID Theft Protection.

Total Care Auto ID Theft Protection is a complimentary service provided to help you alleviate your customer's risk of identity theft. By simply embedding this membership service in a routine sales transaction, you will be effectively able to generate additional revenue for your business, as well as protect your customers from the threats of ID theft.

More than just a credit monitoring service, ID theft protection from Total Care Auto provides relentless 24-hour help and support to keep your client's personal information secure, private and, well, personal. Even in today's highly mobile, highly interactive world, being protected is still possible - and that's peace of mind they can take with them anywhere, anytime.
